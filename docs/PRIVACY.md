# Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Pixel Library collects and processes the minimum account and activity data needed to provide the service.

## Information Collected

- Account information you provide, such as username, email address, first name, and last name
- Profile customization choices, such as selected character/avatar
- Study activity data, including session start and end times, break counts, break durations, and group participation
- Technical information needed to operate the app and API securely

## How Information Is Used

- To create and manage your account
- To authenticate you and secure access to the app
- To store and display your study history and aggregate totals
- To power invite-only study groups and related collaboration features
- To monitor service reliability and protect against abuse

## Sharing

- Pixel Library does not sell personal data.
- Data may be processed by infrastructure providers used to host the app and database.

## Retention

- Account and activity data are retained until you delete your account or the service removes inactive data under a documented retention policy.

## Account Deletion

- Users can delete their account from within the app.
- Deletion removes the user account and related data as implemented by the backend.

## Security

- Reasonable technical safeguards are used to protect stored data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.
